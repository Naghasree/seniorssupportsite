Questions to Ask Before Hiring a Caregiver

Hiring the right caregiver can improve safety and quality of life. The interview process should focus on experience, communication, trust, and emergency readiness.

Experience and Skills

Ask about care experience with mobility support, memory care, bathing assistance, medication reminders, and meal preparation. Confirm comfort level with your family member's specific needs.

Scheduling and Reliability

Clarify availability, backup coverage, punctuality expectations, and holiday/weekend coverage. A strong plan for coverage gaps is essential.

Safety and Emergencies

Ask how the caregiver handles falls, medication concerns, confusion episodes, and emergency calls. Verify whether they have CPR or first-aid training.

Communication

Define how updates will be shared with family members, including daily notes, medication updates, and appointment follow-up information.

Interview Checklist

  • Ask scenario-based safety questions
  • Request references and verify them
  • Confirm legal employment and background checks
  • Review responsibilities in writing
  • Start with a trial period when possible
