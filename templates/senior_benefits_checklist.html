Benefits Guide

Senior Benefits Checklist by State

Many families miss support programs simply because they do not know where to look. Use this checklist to review key benefit categories and build a state-by-state action plan.

Healthcare and Insurance Support

Start with Medicare and supplemental coverage options. Review prescription assistance programs and local counseling services that explain plan choices during enrollment windows.

Food and Nutrition Programs

Check eligibility for SNAP, local meal delivery, and senior center meal programs. Community organizations often provide additional grocery and nutrition support.

Transportation and Mobility

Look for county paratransit, non-emergency medical transport, and volunteer ride programs. Transportation support can directly improve appointment follow-through.

Utility and Housing Assistance

Many states and counties offer help with electricity, heating, rent support, or weatherization programs for older adults on fixed incomes.

Checklist to Complete This Week

  • List current income, insurance, and monthly costs
  • Search state aging services website
  • Call local Area Agency on Aging office
  • Document deadlines and required forms
  • Track submitted applications in one file
